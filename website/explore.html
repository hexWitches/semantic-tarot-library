Explore

Discover the intricate web of creators, illustrators, and archetypes that define the Tarot tradition. Here, we move beyond the single card to examine the systemic connections between decks and the people who brought them to life. Delve into specialized insights on iconography, suit history, and the shifting meanings of the cards as they traveled through history.

About the project

The SMT Library is designed to preserve and navigate the complex iconography and history of Tarot through Linked Open Data (LOD). This project was developed for the Semantic Digital Libraries course (a.y. 2025/2026) within the Digital Humanities and Digital Knowledge Master's Degree at the University of Bologna.
