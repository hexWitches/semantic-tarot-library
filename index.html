The SMT Library

The Semantic Tarot Library is a digital ecosystem dedicated to the organization and study of tarot decks and their rich iconography. By integrating Linked Open Data (LOD), it models the intricate relationships between cards, decks, creators, and symbolic motifs, providing a structured environment for exploration and interpretation.

About the project

The SMT Library is designed to preserve and navigate the complex iconography and history of Tarot through Linked Open Data (LOD). This project was developed for the Semantic Digital Libraries course (a.y. 2025/2026) within the Digital Humanities and Digital Knowledge Master's Degree at the University of Bologna.
